# Text Elements
Important thing to understand here is
a loop placed inside this shall rotate because of 
Lorentz law of force, NOT for some silly people say that 
the wire forms a kind of magnet that gets attracted 
to the magnet that created by 3 phase color wires.

Simply imagine if a loop kept in between that 3 phase wires,
and you slowly increase only one say yellow coils current, causing
 magnetic field to slowly increase,
this change in magnetic field shall cause
current to induce in the coil in the direction that 
causes Bind to oppose Borignal by Lenz's law
so the current induced will be as shown 
and so now
this current flow present in Borginal magnetic
field liable to lorentnz force law, 
causing force to act as shown 
 and so coil will move and comes to the position 
shown in the second figure, now  
as the coil is in such a way that magnetic flux
through the coil is zero, so induces NO current in the loop
so the force acting stops
but we don't want that we want the coil to rotate 
continously, so what we do is
we place another coil of blue in such a way that when force because of yellow gets to zero
current and so force gets induced because of blue magnets' slow increase of magnetic field,
and so the rotation continues

The thing is, this change of magnetic field happens at the rate of current we change and so 
the rotation also happens at that rate, when the coil gets perfectly alighned with the magnetic field 
as shown in second figure, rotation stops but before it reaches that position another coil pushes it forward
so rotation continues, and there's always that gap from actual change of frequency of current
and rotation speed of main coil, because if both matches perfectly rotation stops. - Slightly doubt
 and this current induced will generate somewhat heat also in the motor rotor, but that's fine
